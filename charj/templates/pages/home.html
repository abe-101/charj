The Problem

Credit card issuers close accounts with no activity for 12-24 months. When they do:

  • Your credit utilization ratio goes up
  • Your average account age goes down
  • Your credit score takes a hit

If you have cards you rarely use but want to keep open, you need activity. Charj automates that.

FAQ

Is my card data secure?
Card data is handled entirely by Stripe. Charj never sees or stores card numbers. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified.
What's the minimum charge?
$0.50. That's the smallest amount Stripe can reliably process.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel through your dashboard or Stripe's portal. No fees, no questions.
How many cards can I add?
No limit. Each card gets its own subscription.
Will small charges hurt my credit score?
No. Small recurring charges don't negatively impact your score. Keeping accounts open and active is what matters.
