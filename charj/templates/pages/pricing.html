FAQ

Why do I choose the price?
Different people have different preferences. Some want minimal yearly charges. Others want monthly visibility on their statements. You know what works for you.
What's the minimum?
$0.50. That's the smallest amount Stripe can reliably process.
Is there a maximum?
No practical limit. Though if you're charging yourself $1000/month to keep a card active, you might want to reconsider.
Per card or per account?
Per card. Each card you add gets its own subscription with its own amount and schedule.
Can I change my price later?
Yes. Modify through the Stripe customer portal.
Any hidden fees?
No. You pay what you choose. No setup fee, no account fee, no cancellation fee.
Refunds?
The charges are typically very small and go through successfully. We don't offer refunds for processed charges, but you can cancel anytime to stop future charges.
