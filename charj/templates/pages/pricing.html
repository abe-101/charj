FAQ

Pricing Questions

How much does Charj cost?
You choose your own price! Select any amount starting from $0.50 and pick your billing frequency (daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly). Most users choose $1 per year per card - that's less than the cost of a cup of coffee to protect years of credit history.
Why do I choose the price?
We believe in flexibility. Different users have different needs and comfort levels. Some prefer tiny annual charges, others want monthly visibility. By letting you choose, you get exactly what works for you.
What's the minimum charge amount?
The minimum charge amount is $0.50. This is the smallest amount that Stripe can reliably process while still generating card activity that issuers recognize.
Do I pay per card or per account?
You pay per card. Each credit card you add gets its own subscription with its own amount and schedule. This gives you complete control over each card's activity and cost.
Can I change my price after signing up?
Yes! You can modify your subscription at any time through the Stripe customer portal. Change your amount, frequency, or cancel entirely - it's all under your control.
Are there any refunds?
Since charges are typically very small and go towards legitimate card activity, we don't offer refunds for charges that were successfully processed. However, you can cancel anytime to prevent future charges.
