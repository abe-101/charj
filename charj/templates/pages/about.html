About Charj

Built by a payment systems expert to solve a real problem.

Our Mission

Protecting Your Credit History

Charj was created to solve a frustrating problem that affects millions of credit card holders: unexpected account closures due to inactivity.

Credit card companies routinely close accounts that show no activity for 12-24 months. When this happens, your credit utilization ratio increases, your average account age decreases, and your credit score can drop significantly. Charj prevents this by keeping your cards active with small, automated charges on a schedule you control.

The Founder

Meet Abe Hanoka

Payment Systems & Calendar Expert

Abe Hanoka is a seasoned full-stack developer with deep expertise in Django, Python, and payment systems. As an open source maintainer of dj-stripe since 2024, he has extensive experience integrating Stripe into Django applications.

Abe specializes in two key areas: payment systems and calendar applications. This unique combination of skills made him perfectly positioned to create Charj, a service that combines scheduled automation with secure payment processing.
