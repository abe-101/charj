Common Questions

Quick Answers

How long does setup take?
Most users complete setup in under 5 minutes. Just create an account, add your card(s), and choose your pricing. That's it!
Will this affect my credit score negatively?
No. Small recurring charges do not negatively impact your credit score. In fact, they help by preventing account closures, which WOULD hurt your score by reducing your available credit and average account age.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes! You can cancel any card subscription at any time through your dashboard or Stripe's customer portal. There are no contracts or cancellation fees.
